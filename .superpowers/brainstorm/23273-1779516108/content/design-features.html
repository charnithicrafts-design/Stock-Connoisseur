Intimator Logic & Calendar

The engine that picks stocks and tracks reports.

Logic & UI Preview

1. The Intimator Engine

Performance Score (0-100)

Fundamentals (60%): P/E, EPS Growth, Debt/Equity thresholds.

Technical (40%): Distance from 200-day SMA, RSI oversold/bought status.

Calculated on sync and saved as a static snapshot.

2. Reporting Calendar

Notifications

Events: Quarterly (Q1-Q4) and Annual Reports (AR).

Push: Browser alerts 1 day before and morning of report.

Local "Email": Generates a pre-filled `mailto:` link for your weekly digest.

Key Features

  • Wishlist Split: Separate tabs/views for US (USD) and Indian (INR) markets.
  • Manual Sync: One-tap refresh to fetch yfinance data and update all scores.
  • Calendar View: Standard monthly grid with dots/labels for report dates.
  • Data Persistence: All stock picks and report dates stored in SQLite WASM.
